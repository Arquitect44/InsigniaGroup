About Insignia Group

Three generations of construction excellence in New York City

The Chen Family Legacy

The Chen family has been active in real estate and construction for three generations. Leon Chen entered the construction business in 2005, following in his father's footsteps and building upon decades of family expertise in New York's real estate industry.

Since 2005, Leon has evolved from subcontractor to general contractor to developer. During this time, he has served as both subcontractor and general contractor in the development and completion of over 10 hotels for national chains throughout New York's five boroughs.

In 2007, Leon Chen started Super Plumbing and Building Supply with his partner to support the family's growing construction business. The building supply company is located at 56-12 58th Street in Maspeth, Queens.

Since 2011, Leon Chen has served as a developer, leading the financing and construction of numerous hotels throughout the five boroughs. He has also successfully completed commercial and residential developments in Queens and Brooklyn.

In 2018, Leon achieved a significant milestone by opening his first hotel in Sunset Park, Brooklyn - the Sunset Park Insignia Hotel located at 741 61st Street, featuring 21 medical office units and 138 parking spaces.
